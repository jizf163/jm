It is simpler in structure than an electrostatic precipitator and has lower investment and stable operation. It can also recover the dust that is difficult to recover. A general bag filter is not suitable for purifying gas containing oil mist, condensate, and sticky powder, and it is generally not resistant to high temperatures.

Efficient HEPA

HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Air Filter) means high-efficiency air filter in Chinese. HEPA is divided into five materials: PP (polypropylene) filter paper, glass fiber, composite PP/PET (polyethylene terephthalate, polyester) filter paper, melt blown polyester non-woven fabric, and melt blown glass fiber.

HEPA filter material is made of chemical fiber meltblown or fiber laminated or woven, and its filtration mechanism has the following four types:
(1) The first one is the interception mechanism, which works on large particles.
(2) The second mechanism is the influence of gravity. Particles with small volume and high density will experience a decrease in velocity when passing through a HEPA filter and naturally settle onto the HEPA. This process is somewhat similar to the sedimentation of sediment in water downstream of a river.
(3) The third mechanism of HEPA filters is the influence of airflow. Due to the uneven weaving of HEPA filters, a large number of air vortices are formed, and ultra small particles are adsorbed on the HEPA filter due to the influence of this cyclone, achieving the purpose of filtration.
(4) The fourth filtering mechanism is that ultrafine particles collide with HEPA filter fibers under Brownian motion and are affected by van der Waals forces, resulting in adsorption and filtration. It can be seen that in addition to the first mechanism of intercepting large particles, it utilizes the static electricity at the fiber roots to adsorb small particles and achieve filtration effect.

The 4M-PTFE membrane is a truly disposable filter that can remove PM2.5 and PM10 to 99.7% without having to repeatedly filter unclean indoor air like an indoor air purifier. The effectiveness of the 4M-PTFE membrane is not affected by any environmental factors. Generally, HEPA filters become ineffective in high humidity environments and their performance significantly deteriorates after 3 uses. When encountering outdoor pollution, the people in homes and offices most need clean outdoor air to ventilate their homes or offices. When outdoor air is severely polluted, it is not possible to open windows for ventilation, even though indoor air purifiers can be turned on. However, the carbon dioxide concentration in the room will gradually increase. When the carbon dioxide concentration exceeds a certain level, it can affect breathing because the concentration of carbonic acid in the blood increases, acidity increases, and acidosis occurs. When the carbon dioxide fraction in the air is 1%, people feel stuffy and dizzy. When it is 4-5%, they feel dizzy. Because carbon dioxide is heavier than air and accumulates at low levels, the harm to indoor children is greater when the carbon dioxide concentration is high.
